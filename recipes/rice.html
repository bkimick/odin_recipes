Basmati Rice

Description

Basmati Rice is a healthy carb that can be seasoned and flavored in several ways, and is a great source of fiber.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dried Basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Sea Salt
  • Avocado Oil
  • Grass-fed butter

Steps

  1. On high heat, add 2 cups water and sea salt to large pot
  2. Add 4 cups dried Basmati rice to the pot
  3. Cover large pot, bringing water & rice to a boil
  4. Once boiling, lower heat to medium
  5. Allow rice to simmer for 25-30 minutes without stirring
  6. Check if water has boiled away, adding more if necessary
  7. Once rice has finished cooking, fluff rice with a fork
  8. Serve Basmatic rice with avocado oil or butter
