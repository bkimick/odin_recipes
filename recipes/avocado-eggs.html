Avocado & Scrambled Eggs

Description

Avocado and Scrambled Eggs are a fat and protein-rich breakfast, ideal for those with high-metabolism. This breakfast can be prepared fairly quickly, and is high in Vitamin C and Vitamin A.

Ingredients

  • Ripe, fresh Avocados
  • 3 or 4 large eggs
  • Grass-fed butter
  • Black Pepper
  • Sea Salt

Steps

  1. On low heat, add grass-fed butter in a skillet
  2. Crack open and scramble eggs in a bowl
  3. Add scrambled eggs to the skillet
  4. While eggs cook on low heat, slice the avocado lengthwise into two halves
  5. Pitting the avocado simply by chopping your knife into the pit, and rotating 90 degrees
  6. Use a spoon scraping around the edges to remove the skin from avocado
  7. Slice avocado into bite-sized pieces
  8. Now that the avocado is prepared, return to the skillet and scramble or flip the eggs
  9. Season avocado and eggs with black pepper and salt to taste
